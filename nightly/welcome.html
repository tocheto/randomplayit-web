Getting started with The Random Player !

Welcome to TheRandomPlayer -

your mobile music player that lets you play your music from BOTH your cloud storage(s) AND your local smartphone library (for smartphone users) at the same time. Long gone are the days when you had to use different players for the different cloud providers. Everything is now in one place.Please check F.A.Q for more info on who we are

No more boring stuff!

No playlists, no alphabetical sorting, no fancy album arts, no shuffling, no repeat all. Don't you ever scroll to search for a song you might feel like listening when you see it.

What you get is Randomness!

All you will see on the screen is 10 (+/-) titles of randomly-selected songs. They will move for a while and will disappear. Then you will automatically get the next set of 10(+/-) randomly- selected songs bubbling around. Then...You get the rest.

Customize your Random player

Don't forget to check around the various settings you can customize from the 'Personalize' button

Whatever, how do I start ?
  1. Use the options in the bottom-right corner to load your music from the cloud ('Add music from the Cloud') and/or from your smartphone (*only for the installed app) ('Add your local music library').
  2. Click 'Start Randomness' and see what happens.
  3. If you are getting bored with the background - just change it by selecting another theme from 'Choose a theme' section or upload your own (*coming soon)
  4. Drop us a line to let us know your experience - check the button on the left
